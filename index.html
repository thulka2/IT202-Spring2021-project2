Scrolling Things

A 2D scrolling game themed around the show "Stranger Things".

Game info:

Use the up and down arrow keys to move.

Dodge each demogorgon to gain a point of score.

Each time you hit a demogorgon you lose a life.

Collect a waffle to gain a life.

Every 15 score you increase the level of difficulty.
